I am an early blockchain adopter. A neuroscientist by day and an artist by night.

Art has always been misused as a tool for manipulation throughout history where the victors could sell their own narratives. Never in history was art backed by truth. The emotions that my works concey are often backed by real data: I am currently experimenting with a new art style combining three aspects: realism, autonomy and emotions. Essentially, a data-driven generative art form using photographs. I create more animated pixel art - satirical, abstract and data driven - focussed around world events.

As an early blockchain user and creator with interests in art, technology and analytics, I am happy to share my expertise with you.
